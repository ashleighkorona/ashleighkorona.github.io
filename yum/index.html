MARINARA

fresh tomato sauce

1 Quart
  1. 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  2. 8 garlic cloves, peeled
  3. 1 35-ounce can peeled Italian plum tomatoes, seeded and lightly crushed, with their liquid
  4. 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  5. 1/2 tsp peperoncino
  6. 10 fresh basil leaves, torn into small pieces freshly ground black pepper
  1. Heat the oil in 2 to 3-quart nonreactive saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Whack the garlic cloves with the flat side of a knife, toss them into the oil, and cook until lightly browned, about 2 minutes.
  3. Carefully slide the tomatoes and their liquid into the oil.
  4. Boil and season lightly with salt and peperoncino.
  5. Lower the heat so the sauce is at a lively simmer, and cook, breaking up the tomatoes with a whisk or spoon, until the sauce is chunky and thick, about 20 minutes.
  6. Stir in the basil about 5 minutes before the sauce is finished.
  7. Taste the sauce, and season with salt and pepper if necessary.
